# Character Bible

## Hero: "Bart"

**NOT Bart Simpson.**
**NOT a buff action hero.**

### Visual Description

- **Build**: Normal, average athletic build. Not overly muscular, not skinny. Just a "regular guy".
- **Head**: Bald.
- **Facial Hair**: Light stubble (5 o'clock shadow). **NO** full beard or goatee.
- **Attire**:
  - Top: Plain Black T-Shirt (short sleeves).
  - Bottom: Blue Jeans (standard denim).
  - Shoes: Brown work boots or shoes.
- **Vibe**: Reluctant hero, everyman, "Die Hard" protagonist energy but average physique.

### Palette Strategy

- **Skin**: Tan/Caucasian normal.
- **Hair/Stubble**: Dark grey/brown for stubble texture.
- **Shirt**: Black/Dark Grey highlights (avoid pure black crush).
- **Jeans**: Classic mid-blue.
